Amenities

Enrich Your Stay with
Extraordinary Resort Amenities

  • Food

    Delight in the vibrant and aromatic flavors of South India as our resort's culinary experts skillfully prepare a delectable array of traditional dishes, showcasing the rich heritage and diverse tastes of the region.

  • Bonfire

    Unwind under the starlit sky as you gather around the mesmerizing bonfire at our resort, where laughter, storytelling, and the crackling warmth of the flames create unforgettable moments of camaraderie and relaxation.

  • Bicycle Riding

    Pedal through breathtaking landscapes and immerse yourself in the beauty of nature as you embark on adventurous bicycle rides offered by our resort, providing an invigorating way to explore the surroundings and create cherished memories along the scenic trails.

  • Swimming Pool

    Dive into pure bliss in our pristine swimming pool, where crystal-clear waters beckon, offering a refreshing sanctuary to relax, unwind, and soak up the sun in sheer tranquility, creating unforgettable moments of leisure and rejuvenation.

  • Badminton Court

    Engage in friendly competition and elevate your game on our state-of-the-art badminton court, where swift serves, precise shots, and spirited rallies unfold, fostering an energetic atmosphere that ignites your passion for the sport and creates lasting memories of active enjoyment.

  • Riverside View

    Immerse yourself in nature's picturesque masterpiece as you indulge in the breathtaking riverside view from our resort, where tranquil waters, lush greenery, and serene surroundings create a captivating backdrop that soothes the soul and invites you to embrace pure serenity and tranquility

About

The SUKHAM resort has been inaugurated in the year 27 January, 2024. The SUKHAM resort is approved by the Government of Tamil Nadu tourism, Tamil Nadu. The SUKHAM resort is located in the Irrupukal Valley, Kotagiri from the Kotagiri bus terminal 4.7km just 10 to 15 minutes travel. The SUKHAM resort has 360-degree hill view
